Natural vs Lab-Grown Diamonds

Both are real diamonds — chemically, physically and optically identical. The difference is origin and price. Here's how they compare at Chamuel & Co.

FactorNatural DiamondLab-Grown Diamond
OriginFormed naturally over billions of years, then minedGrown in a lab in weeks, replicating the same process
AppearanceIdentical sparkle & fireIdentical sparkle & fire — indistinguishable to the eye
CertificationGIA certifiedGIA graded
Price (same size & grade)Premium — the rarer choiceAround 30–60% more affordable
Resale / heirloom valueStronger long-term valueLower resale, excellent value for size
Best forTradition, investment, heirloomsBigger stone or higher grade on the same budget

Story
Our Story

Jewellery Is Never
Just Jewellery

"It can be a chapter of a life, a longing, a family's memory."

When I was young, I never quite understood why the elders in my family held their jewellery so dearly. To me, they were beautiful objects — things to admire, perhaps to wear. Nothing more.

Then came the day my grandfather passed.

In that hospital room, in those final quiet moments, he did something I will never forget. He removed the ring he had worn every single day of his life — and placed it gently into the hands of the person he loved most. The necklace, he left for his children.

"In that moment, I finally understood. Jewellery was never just jewellery."

It can be a chapter of someone's life. A longing. A family's memory — something that whispers: I was here. I loved you. Remember me.

My grandmother is still with us today. Every now and then, she passes a piece she has worn for decades to one of us grandchildren. I always receive it carefully, and keep it with the greatest care.

Because I know that one day, these won't just be jewellery. They will be time itself — time that cannot be bought back. Proof that someone existed, that they loved, that they were here. A way of feeling them still beside us.

"This is the belief that Chamuel&Co. was built upon."

We don't want to create pieces that are beautiful for a few years. We want to create pieces that are still being cherished, still being worn, still being passed on — decades from now.

Because the most precious thing in any piece of jewellery has never been the diamond or the gold.

It is the person behind it. And the memory it carries.
